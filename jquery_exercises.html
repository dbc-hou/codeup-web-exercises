Lesson XXXIX

The Superlative Degree, Part I

In this lesson, we will learn how to form and use the superlative degree (-est or most) for adjectives in Declensions I and II. Declension III adjectives will be covered in the next lesson. The lesson will include these subtopics:

  • English translations of superlative adjectives
  • the standard formation of the superlative
  • forming the superlative for adjectives terminating in -ius/-eus
  • the irregular superlative forms of five frequently-used adjectives:
    • bonus/a/um
    • malus/a/um
    • magnus/a/um
    • parvus/a/um
    • multus/a/um
